Accommodation

Guest Accommodation

Discover the perfect place to rest and rejuvenate during your visit to Potchefstroom, North-West.

Our carefully curated selection of recommended accommodations offers a diverse range of options to suit every traveler's preferences.

Whether you're seeking a cozy bed and breakfast or a charming guesthouse, you'll find the ideal retreat to complement your stay.

Explore our handpicked accommodations and make your trip to Potchefstroom unforgettable.


Turffontein Farm

Turffontein Farm is a 2500 ha-farm about 20 km from Potchefstroom.

Price:

Starting at R2,100 per night for 6 people

Anderland - De Oude Melkstal

"Anderland - De Oude Melkstal" is situated on a beautiful farm along the banks of the Mooirivier in Potchefstroom. There are plenty of hiking trails, historical monuments, and natural parks to explore.

Price:

Starting at R950 per night for 2 people

More information


Droombos Plaashuis

"Droombos Plaashuis" is a lovely house on a farm located 29 km outside of Potchefstroom. The house accommodates 8 people and is an ideal getaway from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Price:

Starting at R2,100 per night for 8 people

More information


Province Guesthouse

"Provence Guest House" is located just 7 km outside of Potchefstroom on the R501 in the direction of Carletonville. The farm offers a peaceful atmosphere with large gardens. The guest house features 2 tastefully decorated rooms with a double bed or 2 single beds. Each room has an en-suite bathroom.

Price:

Starting at R650 per night for 2 people
